Frankenstein

Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made as a project for Text as Data II, taught at the University of Antwerp.

Frankenstein is a novel written by Mary Wollstonecraft Shelly. This manuscript has several drafts and it's original is preserved in Bodleian Library. This mini project serves to show few initial pages of draft made by Mary and Percy Shelly; their modification on the pages signified by each of their hand, highlighted by different color with added additional functionalities to the webpage.
